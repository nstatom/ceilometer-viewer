Ceilometer Cloud Viewer

Data Disclaimer

Hourly cloud data (larger bubbles) is based on hourly airport METAR readings which averages the past 30 minutes of data with double weighting for the last 10 minutes of passing sky condition observations.

High rate cloud data (smaller bubbles) is quasi-instantaneous with very little averaging. The resulting data is more prone to overall cloud status mischaracterizations in patchy cloud conditions since the observation is the result of a point measurement.
